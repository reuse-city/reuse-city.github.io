Reuse City

Design Research | Participatory Action Research | Community Building | Open Source | Green Tech

Reuse City is a studio dedicated to applied research, service design and technology development in the fields of circular economy, right to repair, and zero waste policy. Led by Dr Felipe Schmidt Fonseca, Reuse City is based on decades of experience creating community-oriented initiatives of material reuse.

  • Solutions

Material Reuse

It is a consensus that recycling won't be enough to cope with the growing volume of materials being discarded everyday. The Reuse City studio has the mission of creating knowledge, technology and methodologies to increase the reuse of materials through systems-based approaches. It is borne out of a doctoral investigation titled "Generous cities – weaving commons-oriented systems for the reuse of excess materials in urban contexts".

Solutions

Some of our research-backed solutions to promote the reuse of materials through repairs, upcycling, adaptations and recirculation. Learn more about them here.

ThingData

ThingData is an openly governed database about the reuse of goods and materials. If helps diverse stakeholders engaged with repairs, upcycling and recirculation assess the potential value of materials.

Reuse Commons

Reuse Commons is an open toolkit to create local and collaborative reuse systems. It allows communities to establish protocols and create their circular systems.

School of Reuse

Education on the fundamental skills to promote the reuse of materials in cities and regions.

Custom design

Reuse City can help your organisation or region design create custom systems to reduce the impact of waste.
